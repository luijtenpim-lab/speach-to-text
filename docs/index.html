Features

Everything you need to
type faster with your voice

Voxa is built for speed. Hold a key, speak, release — your words are injected directly into whatever app is in focus.

⚡
Hold-to-record

Hold any key you choose — fn, Option, or a custom key — to start recording. Release to stop. It's that instant.

🎯
Works in any app

Text is injected directly into your active window. Slack, Notion, email, terminal — wherever your cursor is.

🔒
100% local & private

Your voice never leaves your Mac. Transcription happens entirely on-device using Apple's Speech Recognition.

🌍
67 languages

Supports over 67 languages with auto-detect. Switch between English, Spanish, French, Dutch and many more.

🤖
Voice agent commands

Trigger AI actions with custom wake phrases. Say "Hey Voxa" to activate your AI agent while dictating.

📊
Usage dashboard

Track your words, time saved, and most-used apps over time. See how much faster you're working.

How it works

Three steps.
That's it.

Voxa is designed to be invisible. You'll forget it's even running — until you realise how much faster you're working.

1
Install & grant permissions

Open the .dmg, drag Voxa to Applications. Grant microphone and accessibility access in System Settings — takes 30 seconds.

2
Hold your hotkey and speak

Click into any text field in any app. Hold your chosen key (default: fn) and start talking naturally.

3
Your words appear instantly

Release the key. Voxa types your transcription directly into the active app — no copy-paste, no switching windows.
